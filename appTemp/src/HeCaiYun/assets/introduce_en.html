一：Installation cartridge

When first used, please insert the core into the pen head. When the ink of the pen core is exhausted, in order to ensure synchronization of writing, please replace the matching core.

二：Connect Smart Pen

1：Press the power button of the smart pen for 3 seconds to turn on the smart pen.


2：Search Bluetooth with your smart pen close to your phone. Make sure Bluetooth is turn on.


3：Connect Bluetooth


4：When the connection is successful, you can see that the green light of the smart pen flashes slowly, and the icon (pen) in the upper right corner of the app turns green.


三：Connection failed

If the connection fails, please check according to the following prompts

1、Put the smart pen close to the mobile phone.
2、Press the power button for a long time to restart the smart pen.
3、Make sure that the smart pen paired with Bluetooth is correct.
